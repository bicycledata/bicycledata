<h1 id="title">Detailed Instructions iPhone</h1>

<p>
    Sorry, the screenshots are currently only available in Swedish. The English translations are maybe not 100 % correct.
</p>

<p>
    1. Go to Settings / Inställningar.
</p>

<img
    src="/static/images/iphone/01_Settings.jpeg"
    alt="iPhone Settings"
    width="250"
/>

<p>
    2. Go to General / Allmänt.
</p>

<img
    src="/static/images/iphone/02_SettingsGeneral.jpeg"
    alt="iPhone Settings > General"
    width="250"
/>

<p>
    3. Go to About / Om.
</p>

<img
    src="/static/images/iphone/03_SettingsGeneralAbout.png"
    alt="iPhone Settings > General > About"
    width="250"
/>


<p>
    4. Click on Name / Namn.<br>
    5. Type in "bicycledata" as name.
</p>

<img
    src="/static/images/iphone/04_SettingsGeneralAboutName.jpeg"
    alt="iPhone Settings > General > About > Name"
    width="250"
/>

<p>
    6. Go back to Settings / Inställningar.<br>
    7. Choose Hotspot / Internetdelning.
</p>

<img
    src="/static/images/iphone/Settings-hotspot.png"
    alt="iPhone Settings Hotspot"
    width="250"
/>

<p>
    8. Under Hotspot / Internetdelning select "allow others to connect" / "tillåt att andra ansluter".<br>
    9. Click on Wifi password / Wifi lösenord.<br>
    10. Set "bicycledata" as password.
</p>

<img
    src="/static/images/iphone/Hotspot.jpeg"
    alt="iPhone Allow Others"
    width="250"
/>

<p>
    When the logger connects to the hotspot, you'll briefly see a green link symbol in the upper left-hand corner.<br>
    The clock remains green while the device is connected to the hotspot.<br>
    Also the Wifi LED on the logger will be lit while the connection persists.
</p>

<img
    src="/static/images/iphone/Hotspot-connected.jpeg"
    alt="iPhone Allow Others"
